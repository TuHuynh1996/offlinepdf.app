FAQ

Does signing upload my files?

No. PDF signing runs locally on your device.

Can I save multiple signatures?

Yes. You can save multiple signatures and reuse them.

Can I place more than one signature on a page?

Yes. You can place multiple signatures on the same page.

Can I import signature images?

Yes. You can import image signatures and place them as stamps.

Does it support digital certificates?

Yes. You can apply digital signatures using .p12/.pfx certificate files.
