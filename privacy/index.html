Privacy Policy

Offline PDF Toolkit is built for local document work. Your files stay on your device, and the app does not collect personal data, analytics, telemetry, or document content.

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Local processing PDF conversion, OCR, signing, redaction, and compression run on your device.
No document upload File names, file contents, and document metadata are not sent to our servers.
No tracking No cookies, advertising identifiers, analytics, telemetry, or automatic crash reporting.

Summary

Offline PDF Toolkit does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device with no internet connection required for core PDF tools.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No file names, file contents, or metadata are transmitted
  • No cookies, tracking pixels, or advertising identifiers
  • No crash reports are sent automatically

Network Usage

Offline PDF Toolkit does not upload documents or send document content to our servers. All PDF processing happens locally on your device.

For licensing, purchase, and restore flows, the app may communicate with your platform store services: Apple App Store or Microsoft Store. These requests are handled by the store platforms and are not used to transmit your document files or file content.

File Access

The app accesses only the files and folders you explicitly select through the file picker or drag and drop. Processed files are saved to the location you choose. No files are copied, cached, or stored in any hidden location by us.

Third-Party Services

Offline PDF Toolkit does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Apple App Store

If you purchase Offline PDF Toolkit through the Mac App Store, Apple may collect purchase and usage data according to its own privacy policy. We do not have access to this data.

Microsoft Store

If you install or purchase Offline PDF Toolkit through Microsoft Store, Microsoft may process purchase, licensing, and account-related data according to its own privacy policy. We do not receive your document content from these store services.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since the app collects no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at tuhuynh.work@outlook.com.vn.
