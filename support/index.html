Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. Offline PDF Toolkit is 100% offline for core PDF tools. Documents are processed locally on your device.

Where are my converted files saved?

By default, output files are saved in the same folder as the original file. You can change this to a custom folder in the conversion settings.

What file formats are supported?

For file-to-PDF conversion: .doc, .docx, .xls, .xlsx, .pptx, .txt, .rtf, .jpg, .png, .jpeg, .webp, .gif, .bmp, .tiff. PDF tools such as OCR, PDF/A, sign, redact, protect, watermark, and organize work with .pdf files.

Can I open password-protected PDFs?

Yes. Click the lock icon on a protected file and enter the password. Once unlocked, you can use all PDF tools on it.

Can I OCR scanned PDFs?

Yes. Use the OCR tool to convert scanned PDFs into searchable PDFs. OCR runs locally on your device.

Can I create PDF/A files?

Yes. Use the PDF/A tool to convert standard PDFs into archive-ready PDF/A output.

Does the app support digital signatures with certificates?

Yes. Digital signing supports certificate files such as .p12 and .pfx.

Is my data collected or sent anywhere?

No. We collect zero data. No analytics, no telemetry, no tracking. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open the app and click "Restore Purchase." On macOS, purchases are tied to your Apple ID. On Windows, entitlement is tied to your Microsoft Store account.

The app is not opening on my Mac. What should I do?

Make sure you're running macOS 12 Monterey or later. If you see a security warning, go to System Settings > Privacy & Security and allow the app to open.
